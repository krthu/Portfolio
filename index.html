About

After many years of working in the family business, where I held key responsibilities in finance, marketing, and technology, I decided to make a significant career change and follow a long-standing passion: programming. This decision came from a desire to challenge myself and grow in a field that has always intrigued me.

Currently, I am studying mobile application development, gaining hands-on experience in various programming languages such as Java, Kotlin, Swift, and JavaScript. I am also developing my skills in web development and version control through tools like Git and GitHub. My goal is to transition into a full-time programming role where I can contribute to meaningful projects, create innovative solutions, and continue expanding my technical expertise.

In addition to my studies, I’m actively working on personal coding projects to reinforce my learning and prepare for real-world applications. I thrive both in team settings and when working independently, always eager to take on new challenges and improve my abilities.

Outside of my professional pursuits, I enjoy spending quality time with my family. One of my favorite activities is watching my children’s floorball matches, which keeps me connected to their lives and provides a welcome balance to my coding endeavors.
